Recipe 1 : Chocolate biscuit pudding

Ingredients

  • Chocolate biscuits - 2 packs
  • Cocoa Powder - 1/4 cup
  • Butter - 1/2 cup
  • Suger - 1/2 cup
  • Milk -1 cup
  • Vanila extract - 1 teaspoon

Cooking Instructions

Step 1: Crush Biscuits into small pieces.

Step 2: In a sourcespan, melt buter over low heat.

Step 3: Add sugar, cocoa powder, and milk to the melted butter. Stir until smooth.

Step 4: Remove the saucepan from heat and add vanilla extract. Mix well.

Step 5: Combine the crushed biscuits with the chocolate mixture. Ensure all biscuit pieces are coated.

Step 6: In a dish, layer the chocolate-biscuit mixture. Alternate with layers of whole biscuits.

Step 7: Refrigerate for at least 3 hours or overnight to allow the pudding to set.

Step 8: Serve chilled, optionally garnished with whipped cream or chocolate shavings.

Enjoy your simple and delicious chocolate biscuit pudding!

Recipe 2 : Marbel pudding

Ingredients

  • 10-12 Marie biscuits
  • Cocoa Powder - 2 tablespoon
  • Custard powder -2 tablespoon
  • Suger - 1/2 cup
  • Milk -2 cups
  • Vanila extract - 1 teaspoon

Cooking Instructions

Step 1: In a saucepan, heat 1.5 cups of milk. Add sugar and stir until dissolved.

Step 2: In a separate bowl, mix custard powder with the remaining 0.5 cup of cold milk to create a smooth paste.

Step 3: Slowly add the custard mixture to the hot milk, stirring continuously. Cook until the custard thickens. Add vanilla extract and mix well. Let it cool.

Step 4: Meanwhile, dip each biscuit in water for a second and layer them at the bottom of a serving dish.

Step 5: Once the custard has cooled, pour half of it over the biscuit layer.

Step 6: Add cocoa powder to the remaining custard, mix well to create chocolate custard.

Step 7: Pour the chocolate custard over the first layer in the dish.

Step 8: Use a skewer or knife to create a marbled effect by gently swirling through the layers.

Step 9: Refrigerate for at least 3 hours or until set.

Serve chilled and enjoy your simple and delicious marble pudding!

Recipe 3 : Caramel pudding

Ingredients

  • Eggs - 4 eggs
  • Suger - 1 cup
  • Milk -2 cups
  • Vanila extract - 1 teaspoon

Cooking Instructions

Step 1: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

Step 2: In a saucepan, melt sugar over medium heat until it turns into a golden caramel color. Pour the caramel into the bottom of a baking dish and tilt to coat the bottom evenly.

Step 3: In a bowl, beat the eggs. Add milk and vanilla extract, and mix well.

Step 4: Strain the egg mixture to remove any lumps and pour it over the caramel in the baking dish.

Step 5: Place the baking dish in a larger pan filled with hot water (water bath).

Step 6: Bake in the preheated oven for about 45 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean when inserted into the center.

Step 7: Allow the caramel pudding to cool, then refrigerate for a few hours or overnight.

Step 8: Once chilled, run a knife along the edges, invert the pudding onto a serving plate, and let the caramel sauce flow over the top.

Slice and serve chilled. Enjoy your delicious caramel pudding!

Recipe 4 : Banana pudding

Ingredients

  • Ripe bananas - 4
  • Instant vanilla pudding mix - 1 box
  • Milk -2 cups
  • Vanila extract - 1 teaspoon
  • Sweetened condensed milk - 1 can
  • Whipped topping - 1 container
  • Vanilla wafer cookies - 1 box

Cooking Instructions

Step 1: In a large bowl, prepare the instant vanilla pudding mix with cold milk according to the package instructions.

Step 2: Add sweetened condensed milk and vanilla extract to the pudding mixture. Stir until well combined.

Step 3: Fold in the whipped topping until smooth and creamy.

Step 4: Slice the ripe bananas and layer them in the bottom of a serving dish.

Step 5: Spoon a portion of the pudding mixture over the bananas, spreading it evenly.

Step 6: Repeat the layers, finishing with a layer of the pudding mixture on top.

Step 7: Crush some vanilla wafer cookies and sprinkle them over the pudding for added texture.

Step 8: Refrigerate the banana pudding for at least 4 hours or until set.

Slice and serve chilled. Enjoy your delicious banana pudding!
